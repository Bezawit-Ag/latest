Our story

Rooted in tradition, plated for today

Family-owned and proudly based in Bahirdar, we spotlight regional ingredients—berbere, mitmita, teff, niter kibbeh (clarified butter), and fresh spices from local markets—served with authentic techniques.

From our hand-made injera to slow-simmered wats, charcoal-grilled tibs, and traditional kitfo, every dish honors recipes passed down through generations while embracing modern presentation.
